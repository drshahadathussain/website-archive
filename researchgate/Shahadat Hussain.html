About me

Dr. Shahadat Hussain is a Mechanical Engineering researcher specializing in 3D printing of carbon fiber–reinforced polymer composites and metamaterial structures, including cubes and beams. His experimental research focuses on flexural and compression testing, mechanical characterization, and structural performance of additively manufactured composite materials for advanced engineering applications.
